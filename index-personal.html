Making finance more accessible, cheaper, and faster.

I have seen, firsthand, the power that finance has on the world. It is a system that can facilitate so much growth, or stifle it just as quickly through abuses. Technology has come a long way, yet modern finance feels old. I believe archaic experiences and philosophies have led to many of our largest institutions falling behind on offering the best financial products. I know there is plenty of room for improvement. That is why I founded Vest.

I am in Chicago. If you’re interested in grabbing some coffee or playing some ping pong while talking about fintech, email me at tripathi@vesthq.com or tweet me @atfintech. I’m always excited to meet fellow designers, developers, and entrepreneurs.
